Beach

My beach has never been introduced to science but I think if it was it would go like:
"Who are you, what are you doing
I am undefinable, I exist for myself
you should not know the names of me
leave them unnamed
I am here for a walk, a swim, fly a kite, kick a ball, sit on the rocks and make a living room out of the uncomfortable bits
drag a net, find an irukandji, rush all the nippers out of the water
but do not call me names
I have a gully in the middle, which is sometimes full and sometimes not
I have mangroves out the back and salt flats next to them
I have an ugly bleaching scummy reef in the middle that you can walk out on
I have bindis in the sand and I laugh when you get them in your grey little feet
I have paths to other parts of me, carved in by BMX bikes
I have tides and lows but do not measure them
I have rocks on either side but do not analyse them
Put me in your mouth and swallow and sunburn and trudge home
Do not try to name me, you'll ruin it"
