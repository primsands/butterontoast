Clumsy weird translation of the poem Taman from Aku Ini Binatang Jalang by Chairil Anwar so I can avoid the work that is ever-pressingly more due

The garden that we both have
isn't very spacious, just small
we wouldn't lose each other in it.
For us it's enough.
The flowers in the garden aren't dozens of colours
The grass isn't anything like a carpet
soft and fine underfoot.
For us it's no obstacle.
Because
in the garden that we both have
You're a flower, I'm a beetle
I'm a beetle, you're a flower.
Our small, sun-full garden
Is a place far removed from the world and mankind

March 1943
